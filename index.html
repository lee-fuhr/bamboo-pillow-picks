How we choose our picks

We base our recommendations on a few honest sources: the long-term feedback of real owners, the consensus among published expert reviews from reputable outlets, and the reputation of each brand for standing behind its product. We look for pillows that don’t develop hard clumps, lose their loft, or suffer from cover pilling within the first year. No one on our team has personally slept on every pillow here, and we don’t claim to have run timed lab tests. Instead, we dig into what actually breaks, zippers that jam, foam that turns brittle, covers that shrink, and favor models with a proven track record of holding up through regular use and washing.
